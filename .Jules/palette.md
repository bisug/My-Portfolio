## 2025-05-15 - Scroll Progress Indicators in Long Portfolio Sites
**Learning:** For long-scrolling, single-page portfolios with 100svh sections, users often lose track of their position. A high-contrast scroll progress bar provides an immediate, "at-a-glance" visual cue that aids orientation without being obtrusive.
**Action:** Implement a Neo-Brutalist styled scroll progress bar that aligns with the existing design system (thick borders, vibrant colors).
