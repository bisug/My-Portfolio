About

Hello! I am Bisu Ghalan, a dedicated Cybersecurity Student deeply fascinated by the evolving world of digital defense and ethical hacking. My journey began with a curiosity about how networks operate beneath the surface, leading me to explore everything from vulnerability assessments to secure systems architecture.

Currently, I am pursuing my Cyber Security & Network Technology (BCS CSNT) degree at Lincoln International College. This academic foundation has provided me with invaluable insights into cryptographic principles, threat modeling, and network infrastructure management. Whether I'm participating in Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges or analyzing network packets, I approach every technical problem with an analytical mindset and a strong commitment to continuous growth. Based in Kathmandu, Nepal, I am constantly seeking opportunities to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world security scenarios.
